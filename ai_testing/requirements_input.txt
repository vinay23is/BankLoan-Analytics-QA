REQ-01: The Bad Loan Rate (NPA proxy) must equal the count of Charged Off loans
divided by total loan applications, expressed as a percentage rounded to 2 decimal places.

REQ-02: All loan amounts must be positive values greater than zero.

REQ-03: All interest rates must fall between 5% and 30% inclusive for standard
retail lending products.

REQ-04: Loan grade must be one of A, B, C, D, E, F, or G.
No other values are permitted in the grade column.

REQ-05: Debt-to-income ratio (dti) must be a non-negative number not exceeding 100.
